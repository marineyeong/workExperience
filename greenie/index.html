Our Strengths

End-to-end Greenie Recycling ProcessFrom waste collection to recycled product manufacturing

We can produce high-quality recycled products through our systematic recycling process.

  • 1

    Collect Plastic Waste

    A vast amount of recyclable plastic waste is secured from our partners across Asia
  • 2

    Break Down Into Flakes

    Plastic waste is fed into shredders, which break it down into much smaller pieces
  • 3

    Transform Into Recycled Pellets

    The shredded plastic is melted and crushed together to form pellets
  • 4

    Manufacture Recycled Products

    Recycled products are developed and produced with thorough R&D of plastic properties

Research and DevelopmentGreenie Lab
